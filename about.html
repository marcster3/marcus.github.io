My Interests

Data Science & Analytics

With the rise of AI representation in the data space is becoming paramount. Something very important to me is responsibly training data on datasets that represent everyone. Having diversity in AI management is the only way for AI to treat the people it is trying to help fairly. In the future I could see myself as part of a team helping to manage AI and train it on socially consious data.

Film

Since I was a child I have always loved watching movies. Coming into adulthood, that love has only grown. Through my time at Cornell I am now minoring in film and expanding my interest into film theory and how film can be valuable beyond entertainment. I have found its connections to literature, art, science, psychology, and philosophy as avenues to explore film in an intelectual way. I have made one film, Breakfast for Two linked on the Projects tab and have been a part of many other films from other students. Now understanding the aspects of the filmmaking process and its potential as an academic and educational tool my appreciation for film will only grow.
